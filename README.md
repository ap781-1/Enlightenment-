# 🕯️ Enlightenment - A Mixed Blessing



## About the Essay

This essay explores the paradox of the **Enlightenment** during the **French Revolution**. While the Enlightenment promised reason, rationality, science, and human freedom, the revolutionary era also witnessed extreme violence during the **Reign of Terror**. 

The essay argues that Enlightenment should not be seen simply as a prelude to the revolution, but as something that **actively unfolded during the revolutionary moment itself**. 

Ultimately, the essay investigates the tension between **reason and violence**, asking whether the Enlightenment created the conditions for democratic emancipation or inadvertently opened the door to ideological extremism.
